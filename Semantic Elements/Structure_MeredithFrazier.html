Miniature Dachshunds

All about miniature dachshunds and why everyone should have one!

Dachshunds are a small breed of dog, typically between 16 and 33 pounds. Within the dachshund breed, there are multiple varieties, not just in coloring, but in size: the standard and the miniature. In fur type, common coat types are wirehaired, smooth, and longhaired.

With multiple types of the same breed, it can be difficult to know what the best type is. Miniature dachshunds are typically between 8 to 11 pounds and 5 to 7 inches tall. Smooth dachshunds and long haired dachshunds tend to be the softest to pet. Common coat colors are red-haired, chocolate, tan, and black.

Dachshunds tend to be loud dogs. They do not normally like strangers, and they are very protective of their family. They love to be around their owners and are very friendly to kids.
